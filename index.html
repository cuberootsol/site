Questions We Get Asked

Straight answers, no jargon.

What's a fractional CTO?
A fractional CTO is a senior technology leader who works with your business part-time. You get the strategic thinking, vendor management, and architecture decisions of a full-time CTO — without the $200K+ salary. Most of our clients need 10-20 hours per month of CTO-level work.
We already have an IT person. Why do we need you?
Your IT person is great at keeping the lights on — help desk tickets, password resets, hardware setup. We're the strategic layer above that. We design the systems, choose the tools, plan the roadmap, and make sure technology is driving revenue. We make your existing IT person more effective, not redundant.
How is this different from an MSP?
MSPs provide monitoring and break-fix support — they react when things go wrong. We're proactive. We design your technology strategy, build custom tools, optimize cloud spend, and align everything to business goals. Think of an MSP as a mechanic and us as the engineer who designs the engine.
What industries do you work with?
Healthcare, professional services, manufacturing, retail, tech startups — we work across industries. Our approach is business-first, not industry-first. If your business runs on technology, we can help. We adapt our compliance knowledge to your regulatory requirements (HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI).
What does the Founding Client Program include?
Your first engagement at 50% off — whether that's a technology audit, cloud migration, custom software build, or fractional CTO retainer. Full scope, no shortcuts. In return, we publish a case study about the engagement (anonymized or named, your choice). You also get priority scheduling and a permanent rate lock.
How quickly can you start?
Most engagements begin within 1-2 weeks of the discovery call. For urgent situations (server down, critical system failure), we can start within 48 hours. The discovery call is free and takes about 30 minutes.
