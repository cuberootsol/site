# CUBEROOT SOLUTIONS: SERVICE CATALOG

Professional security advisory and compliance services designed to build resilient organizations.

---

## 1. STRATEGIC LEADERSHIP

### vCISO (Virtual Chief Information Security Officer)
Executive-level security leadership provided on a fractional basis. Ideal for startups and mid-market firms needing strategic guidance without the cost of a full-time hire.
- **Includes:** Board reporting, budget planning, security roadmap development, and team mentorship.

### Security Program Development
Building a security organization from the ground up or maturing an existing one.
- **Includes:** Policy creation, control implementation, and maturity assessments against NIST CSF or ISO 27001.

---

## 2. COMPLIANCE & GOVERNANCE

### Compliance Readiness (SOC2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, NIST)
End-to-end support to prepare your organization for successful audits.
- **Includes:** Gap analysis, remediation tracking, evidence collection, and liaison with auditors.

### Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM)
Identifying and managing the risks posed by your vendors and partners.
- **Includes:** Vendor risk assessments, contract reviews, and ongoing monitoring.

---

## 3. TECHNICAL ADVISORY

### Security Architecture Review
Deep-dive analysis of cloud, network, and application architectures to identify structural security flaws.
- **Includes:** Threat modeling, data flow analysis, and "Secure by Design" recommendations.

### Incident Response Planning
Preparing your team for the inevitable.
- **Includes:** IR Plan development, Tabletop Exercises (TTX), and communication playbooks.

---

## 4. ENABLEMENT

### Security Awareness Training
Building a security-conscious culture.
- **Includes:** Phishing simulations, executive training, and developer-focused secure coding workshops.

### Compliance Automation Advisory
Guidance on selecting and implementing GRC/compliance automation platforms (e.g., Vanta, Drata).
