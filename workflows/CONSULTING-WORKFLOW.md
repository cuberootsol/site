# CUBEROOT SOLUTIONS: CONSULTING LIFECYCLE

This document outlines the standard engagement workflow for advisory and vCISO services at CubeRoot Solutions.

---

## 1. STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT

### 1.1 Discovery & Needs Assessment
- **Activities:** Initial consultation to understand Client’s industry, regulatory requirements (SOC2, HIPAA, etc.), and current security maturity.
- **Deliverable:** High-level Gap Analysis or Needs Summary.

### 1.2 Proposal & Roadmapping
- **Activities:** Define the scope of the advisory (Retainer vs. Project). Outline the 6-12 month security roadmap.
- **Deliverable:** Consulting Proposal and Retainer Schedule.

### 1.3 Contracting
- **Activities:** Execution of the `CONSULTING-AGREEMENT.md` and initial SOW/Retainer.
- **Deliverable:** Signed Agreement and First Invoice.

---

## 2. ONBOARDING & EXECUTION

### 2.1 Deep-Dive Onboarding
- **Who:** Lead Consultant + Client Stakeholders (IT, HR, Engineering).
- **Activities:** Review of existing policies, architecture diagrams, and previous audit results. Granting of necessary access.
- **Timeline:** Week 1-2.

### 2.2 Security Program Baseline
- **Activities:** Establish the "Current State" baseline against a chosen framework (NIST CSF, ISO 27001).
- **Deliverable:** Maturity Baseline Report.

---

## 3. CONTINUOUS ADVISORY (RETAINER CADENCE)

### 3.1 Monthly Cadence
- **Activities:**
  - Monthly Strategy Call.
  - Review of recent security events or changes.
  - Progress check on roadmap items.
- **Deliverable:** Monthly Status Email.

### 3.2 Ongoing Support
- **Activities:** Ad-hoc advisory on security questions, vendor reviews, and architectural changes as hours permit.

### 3.3 Quarterly Business Review (QBR)
- **Activities:** Formal review of the quarter’s progress. Adjustment of the roadmap for the next quarter.
- **Deliverable:** QBR Presentation Deck.

---

## 4. RENEWAL & EVOLUTION

### 4.1 Annual Program Review
- **Activities:** Comprehensive review of the year’s achievements. Risk assessment update.
- **Deliverable:** Annual Security Report.

### 4.2 Agreement Renewal
- **Activities:** Review of retainer hours and fees. Signature of renewal SOW.
- **Timeline:** 60 days prior to agreement expiration.

---

## DECISION GATES
- **Gate 1 (Post-Discovery):** Client agrees to the proposed roadmap.
- **Gate 2 (Post-Baseline):** Client approves the prioritized list of remediation tasks.
- **Gate 3 (Quarterly):** Client confirms satisfaction with advisory value and roadmap progress.
