This Consulting Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [EFFECTIVE_DATE] (the "Effective Date") by and between:

**CubeRoot Solutions LLC**, with a principal place of business at [COMPANY_ADDRESS] ("Consultant"), and 

**[CLIENT_NAME]**, a [STATE_OF_INCORPORATION] corporation ("Client").

## 1. SERVICES
Consultant shall provide security advisory and strategic consulting services, which may include vCISO (Virtual Chief Information Security Officer) services, compliance readiness (SOC2, ISO 27001), and security program development. Specific activities and deliverables will be defined in a Statement of Work ("SOW") or Retainer Schedule.

## 2. ENGAGEMENT MODEL
Consultant offers services on a retainer or fixed-project basis.
- **Project-Based:** Specific deliverables with defined start and end dates.
- **Retainer-Based:** Ongoing advisory services provided for a recurring monthly or quarterly fee.

## 3. FEES AND PAYMENT
**3.1 Fees:** As specified in the SOW or Retainer Schedule.
**3.2 Payment Terms:** Retainer fees are invoiced in advance on the first of each month and are due Net-30.
**3.3 Late Payments:** Overdue balances are subject to a late fee of 1.5% per month.

## 4. IP OWNERSHIP
**4.1 Client Materials:** Client retains all rights to its data and pre-existing materials.
**4.2 Deliverables:** Upon full payment, Consultant assigns to Client all rights to custom reports, policies, and strategy documents created specifically for Client.
**4.3 Consultant IP:** Consultant retains ownership of its methodologies, frameworks, and templates used to deliver the services.

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to protect each other’s Confidential Information with a reasonable degree of care. This includes technical data, business strategies, and security program details.

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION
**6.1 Term:** This Agreement continues until terminated by either party.
**6.2 Termination:** Either party may terminate this Agreement or any active SOW upon thirty (30) days' written notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay for all services rendered up to the termination date.

## 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Consultant’s total liability for any claim arising under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client to Consultant in the six (6) months preceding the claim.

## 8. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR
Consultant is an independent contractor, not an employee or agent of Client. Consultant is responsible for all taxes and insurance for its personnel.
